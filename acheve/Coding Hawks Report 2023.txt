About:

Coding Hawks Triumphs at Falling Walls Lab 2023: A Global Achievement:   (July-Nov, 2023)
🌐 National to International Success (3rd July, 2023 @ Falling walls lab Lahore):

In the dynamic world of coding and innovation, where breakthrough ideas are the currency of progress, Coding Hawks has once again demonstrated its prowess on the global stage. We are thrilled to share the remarkable journey of Asma Rehman, a dedicated member of our community, and Dr. Nadeem Iqbal, the Club In-charge of Coding Hawks. Their project, "AI-based Bee Hive," secured 1st place nationally and represented our university on the global stage.





🌍 International Debut in Berlin (7th Nov, 2023 @ Falling walls lab Berlin):
With their national victory, Asma and Dr. Nadeem earned the golden ticket to the global finale in Berlin, Germany. The Falling Walls Lab provided them with a stage to present their solutions to pressing global challenges, with just three minutes to captivate a jury of experts and the worldwide audience. While the ultimate title eluded them, their participation was an unparalleled achievement, marking the first-ever representation of our university at an international level.








Coding Hawks Cultivates Success at Venture Spark (Nov 29, 2023):

Special Prize at Venture Spark of Rupees 10,000:
Mariyam Pervez, a brilliant member of Coding Hawks, sowed the seeds of innovation with her project titled "Automatic Weed Detection and Spraying Robot." Under the guidance of Dr. Nadeem Iqbal, Mariyam participated in the prestigious business idea competition, Venture Spark. Mariyam Pervez's success at Venture Spark adds another feather to Coding Hawks' cap. It showcases our commitment to fostering innovation and empowering our members to create solutions that not only excel in technical prowess but also hold immense practical value.



Venture Spark Prize Nominee:
Being shortlisted for the Special Prize at Venture Spark comes with a reward-a chance to win Rupees 10,000. This acknowledgment not only highlights Mariyam's innovation but also positions Coding Hawks as a hub for impactful and commercially viable ideas. As we celebrate Mariyam's achievement, we look forward to the continued growth of Coding Hawks as a breeding ground for inventive minds. Mariyam's Automatic Weed Detection and Spraying Robot is a glimpse into the future of sustainable agriculture.





Workshop on Mobile Application Development Instagram Clone using React Native (April, 2023): 
The Coding Hawks workshop on React Native, held from May 15th to May 17th, 2023, was a resounding success. Participants gained in-depth knowledge of React Native development and acquired essential skills to build cross-platform mobile applications. From understanding the fundamentals of React and setting up the development environment to mastering folder structure and designing reusable components, attendees were equipped with the tools needed to create clean, scalable, and visually appealing interfaces. The workshop's interactive exercises and realworld tasks provided hands-on practice, reinforcing the concepts covered. The student challenge encouraged participants to showcase their skills and creativity, fostering a sense of healthy competition. Congratulations to all the participants for their dedication and enthusiasm throughout the workshop. They are now well-prepared to excel in their future mobile app development endeavors.

Workshop on DevOPs (January, 2023): 
The DevOps Workshop provided participants with the necessary tools and techniques to embrace DevOps practices and drive efficiency and innovation within their organizations. The attendees left the workshop equipped with the knowledge and skills to implement DevOps methodologies, automate workflows, enhance collaboration, and deliver high-quality software products at an accelerated pace. Real-world case studies and interactive exercises allowed participants to apply their newly acquired knowledge in practical scenarios. They tackled challenges related to configuration management, deployment pipelines, and monitoring, honing their problem-solving skills and gaining confidence in their ability to address real-world DevOps challenges.
